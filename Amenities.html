About the Area
A lovely two bedroom/one bath home with a beautiful deck and screened porch. The house is just 25 feet from the ocean. This house sleeps 4 and is enveloped on the sides and back with trees. The secluded location is on a private road which extends more than a half mile from the town road. From the deck you watch lobster boats and all kinds of sail boats, gorgeous sunsets, and when the water is still, a frequent friend, a Great Blue Heron commands the waterfront. In the morning, over coffee on the porch, you may see an osprey in the 100 foot high nest next door.
