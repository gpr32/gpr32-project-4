About the Area
The Glenmere Ocean House sits just 30 feet from the water on the lower west side of the St. George Peninsula, 15 miles south of Rockland, overlooking a wide expanse of ocean where the St. George River meets Muscongus bay and the Atlantic Ocean.The secluded location is on a private road which extends more than a half mile from the town road. Yet, it is only 5 minutes to the Monhegan Island Passenger Ferry and Mail boat in Port Clyde, 45 minutes to Boothbay Harbor, 30 minutes to beautiful Camden, and 20 minutes to Summer festivals in Rockland. Picnic at the beautiful and famous Marshall Point Lighthouse not far away or explore Port Clyde and discover everything this gorgeous area has to offer.
